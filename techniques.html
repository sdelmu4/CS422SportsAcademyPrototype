Soccer Techniques

In this section we will be discussing about the different techniques that are used in soccer. Some of the most common ones are dribbling, passing, and juggling.

Click on the items below to learn more about each section:

Dribbling

Some of the techniques for dribbling are cone dribbling, step-over, and feint

Cone dribbling

Cone dribbling is a type of dribbling technique used in practice that you would make you use a cone in the middle of their area and have players be able to have the ball be dribbled towards the cone and come back. Other times the player would dribble past the cone and swap places with another player while dribbling the ball.

Step-Over

This type of dribbling would be used to trick the opponent with where the ball is going to go using a circular motion on the ball while facing off the opponent. You kick the ball with your opposite foot pushing the ball in another direction and accelerating.

Feint

This type of technique is used when you are close to the goal and make it seem like you are going to hit a shot. You pretend to shoot the ball but you either pull the ball away or side step to have the defender fooled.

Passing

Passing is an essential fundamental for soccer and would need to know how to pass to try and win the game. Some of these passing techniques are as followed

Short pass

This type of pass is used when trying to pass it someone that is close to you. It is used when trying to make a quick decision and exchanges to other players in your team in tight spaces.

Long pass

Using a long pass would be used when trying to get the ball as far away from your goalie to prevent a goal. Normally with this you would need to do kick the ball high in the air to prevent the enemy team from getting the ball.

Juggling

This type of technique involves not just your feet but also parts of your body to control the ball. Some of these techniques you would use are:

Head Juggling

Head Juggling is used for keeping the ball in the air with just using your forehead. Head juggling is practiced to be able to do headers either for passing to another player or be able to shoot at the goal with your head.

Thigh Juggling

Thigh Juggling is used to have control over the ball when it is in the air. In game this can be used to maintain control over the ball when the ball is in the air using a long pass. You can also be able to pass using your thighs as well.
