Introduction

FIFA, World Cup, Futbol, soccer. You've probably heard it everywhere. As one of the most popular sports in the entire world, and millions of fans worldwide, it's no surprise that you find yourself here now.

In this crash course, you will learn the basic rules you need to know in soccer. You will also be introduced to the techniques you will soon be practicing. And lastly, you will become informed about injuries and how to prevent them in soccer.
