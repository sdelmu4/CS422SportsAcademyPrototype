All About Soccer

Soccer is game with two teams of players, each competing to score by kicking a ball into the goal post. Teams conist of 11 members on the field, each fulfilling a roll as an attacker or defender on the field.

Do's and Don'ts

In soccer, you have to play it a certain way, and there are things you can and can't do

  • Move the ball using your feet
  • No using your hands unless you are the goalie
  • Avoid foul play, fouls
  • Be a good sport
